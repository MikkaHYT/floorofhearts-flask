Our Story

Floor of Hearts is a family-run business with over 20 years of experience in the flooring industry. We take pride in offering a personalized service from start to finish, ensuring that every customer finds the perfect flooring solution for their home or business.

Our journey began when founder Luke Hearts recognized the need for a flooring company that combines quality products with exceptional customer service. Since then, we have grown to become a trusted name in Swindon and the surrounding areas.

We believe that flooring is not just a practical necessity but an essential design element that can transform any space. That's why we offer a wide range of high-quality flooring options, from luxury vinyl tiles to engineered wood, carpets, laminates, and more.

Our Commitment

  • Quality Products: We source our products from reputable manufacturers to ensure durability and longevity.
  • Expert Advice: Our team has extensive knowledge and experience in all types of flooring, allowing us to provide expert guidance to our customers.
  • Professional Installation: We offer professional installation services to ensure your flooring is fitted to the highest standard.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your satisfaction is our priority, and we go above and beyond to meet your expectations.
